Delete Your Account

Permanently remove your account and all associated data

You can delete your Cruxle account at any time directly from within the app. Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete from the App

Follow these steps inside the Cruxle app:

1
Open the Cruxle app and go to the Profile tab.
2
Tap Settings.
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
4
Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account will be permanently removed.
Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile, game history, streak, group memberships, and all personal data. This action cannot be reversed. In-app purchases (such as Remove Ads) are tied to your store account and are not refunded upon deletion.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed within 30 days:

✓
Your profile — display name, username, and email
✓
All game history, scores, and streaks
✓
Group memberships and leaderboard data
✓
Hint balance and notification preferences

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to log in or access the app to delete your account, contact us by email and we will delete your account and data manually within 30 days.
